Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Kids Task Tracker ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our App.

This App is designed for children and their parents/guardians. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and other applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use Kids Task Tracker, you may provide us with the following information:

  • Child's Name: Used to personalize the app experience and display task assignments
  • Task Information: Details about tasks, chores, and activities created by parents/guardians
  • Points and Rewards: Progress tracking and achievement data
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and password (encrypted)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

We may automatically collect limited technical information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (feature usage, time spent)
  • Crash Reports: Error logs to improve app stability (no personal data included)

We do NOT collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Camera or photo library access
  • Microphone or audio recordings
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Biometric data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To enable core features like task creation, tracking, and reward management
  • Personalization: To customize the experience for each child and parent
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app features
  • Safety: To detect and prevent fraud or security issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

By default, all data in Kids Task Tracker is stored locally on your device. This means:

  • Your child's tasks, points, and profile information remain on your phone/tablet
  • No data is transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync
  • You have full control over your data

4.2 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data
  • Secure transmission protocols (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Regular security audits
  • No sharing of data with third parties (except as required by law)

4.3 Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained on your device until you delete the app or clear app data
  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active; deleted upon account deletion

5. Parental Consent and Control

5.1 Parental Consent

Parents or guardians must provide verifiable consent before their child's personal information is collected or used. We obtain this consent through:

  • Account creation by a parent/guardian
  • Explicit opt-in for any data collection beyond local storage
  • Clear disclosure of what information is collected and how it is used

5.2 Parental Controls

Parents/guardians can:

  • View all information collected about their child
  • Request correction or deletion of data
  • Disable data collection features
  • Delete the account and all associated data at any time

6. Third-Party Services

Kids Task Tracker does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar tracking tools
  • Third-party advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • External data brokers

7. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties, except:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety: To protect the safety and security of our users
  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who help us operate the app (all bound by confidentiality agreements)

8. Children's Privacy Rights

Children and their parents/guardians have the right to:

  • Access all personal information we hold
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate information
  • Opt-out of optional data collection
  • Request a copy of their data in a portable format
  • Delete their account and all associated data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

9. International Users

If you are located in the European Union, United Kingdom, or other jurisdictions with privacy laws:

  • We comply with GDPR and equivalent regulations
  • You have additional rights including data portability and the right to be forgotten
  • We conduct Data Protection Impact Assessments for all data processing activities
  • We have appointed a Data Protection Officer (contact information available upon request)

10. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Delete personal information collected from you
  • Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

11. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending an email notification to your registered email address
  • Requiring your consent before implementing material changes

Your continued use of the app after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
